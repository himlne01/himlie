The Beginning

Fangirling: when smiling like a fool, laughing hysterically, shortness of breath, fainting, highpitched noises, shaking, fierce head shaking, hyperventilation, endless blogspots, sleeping late at night, etc. occurs as a reaction to the sight or existence of the object of affection and/or obsession.

Fanboy: a passionate fan of various elements of geek culture or any culture.

I should probably just warn you about the dangers ahead of you. The sarcasm will hit you. The feeling of dread and sorrow will possess your soul when you do not understand a reference. And then there are the rants.
